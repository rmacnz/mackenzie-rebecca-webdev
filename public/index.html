View Project: Runescape Marketplace

The Runescape Marketplace is a MEAN stack website where players of the MMORPG Runescape can go to buy and sell items from within the game. Users can register with their username from the game to create and respond to buy/sell offers with items they have access to in the game. It can also be used to search for in game items even without logging in to the site. Click this link to explore the application.

The WebAppMaker is a MEAN stack website which allows you to create and modify simple websites. These websites can contain text, images, videos, and more. You can log in using Facebook or Google or register an account for the website with a new username. Once logged in you can view your websites and edit their pages, including adding, sorting, and editing the widgets on these pages. Click this link to view the latest update to this application.
